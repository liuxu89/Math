Gamma和Beta函数

$\Gamma$函数

\[\Gamma(\alpha) := \int_0^{+\infty} t^{\alpha-1} e^{-t} \mathrm{d} t \quad (\alpha > 0)\]

满足

\[\Gamma(1) = 1; \quad \Gamma(\frac{1}{2}) = \sqrt{\pi} \\ \Gamma(\alpha+1) = \alpha \Gamma(\alpha); \quad \Gamma(n) = (n-1)!\]
